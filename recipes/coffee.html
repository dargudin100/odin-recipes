Here we have an image of a Sandwich. This is a dish that people really like to eat when trying to save money and get full. The sandwich is very intresting because of the fact that it can be made in so many different ways. One thing that people like to eat alongside their sandwich is chips.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Mayo
  • Choice of meat
  • Lettcue

Steps

  1. Start by grabbing two pieces of bread and sitting them flat on a surface
  2. Next I like to add mayo to one side of the bread
  3. On the same side of the bread that you added the mayo, add your slice of cheese and as much of your choice of meat that you would like.
  4. After that put your piece of lettcue on top of the meat
  5. Finally put your other piece of bread on top and you have the option to cut the sandwich for easier digestion.
