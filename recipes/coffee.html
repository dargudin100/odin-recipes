Coffee

Description

Here we have coffee. Fun fact coffee is actually a bean that is grinded down and then brewed in many different ways. Coffee is something that is fascinating because of the fact that it can be enjoyed in so many different ways, shapes and forms. My favorite part about coffee has to be the smell. Half of the taste is in the smell.

Ingredients

  • Coffee mix
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Creamer
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Coffee can be made in so many different ways so the steps can vary based on your brewing method for this example we are going to do the pour over method.
  2. First boil some water. The water does not need to be bubbling when you start to brew.
  3. Add you coffee grounds. Depending on the amount of water you use will depend on the amount of grounds you will add.
  4. Pour hot water into the coffee grounds and let drip into cup
